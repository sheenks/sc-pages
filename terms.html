Terms and Conditions

  • Ability to learn

    Every individual has his/her own capacity and pace of learning. The student’s swimming instructor will adjust accordingly.

  • Payment Method

    Full Payment is required to be deposited or through fund transfer to our bank account. We will acknowledge the payment verification by message reply and confirmation. (see details in payment options).

  • Refunds

    Enrollment fee is non-refundable once the student started his/her first session. Enrollment transaction may be transferred to another person given that no session has been availed yet.

  • Consent for Documentation

    Photographs and videos may be taken. To be utilized in all media forms, for promotional purposes and for the student’s own use.

  • Swim Attire

    All swimmers should have goggles and be dressed in proper swim attire; Swim Trunks and/or rashguard for boys and rashguard/swimsuit and swimming cap for girls. Goggles is a must.

  • Pool venues

    Entrance fee to the pool venue is excluded from the learning fee. Our venues we use may be closed for events/reservations or on holidays, sometimes without prior notice. This is beyond our control. Cancellations of classes due to this reason can be considered for arrangement of another alternative session or change of venue.

  • Schedule and Punctuality

    Students should provide their schedule for the 8 sessions already. Availability of chosen schedules are on a first come first serve basis. A student is ALLOWED to have a MAX of 3 CHANGES of SCHEDULES ONLY, and it should be requested at least 3 days prior. Failure to comply would mean one counted session for date of the missed session. Lessons will end at the scheduled time regardless of start time.

  • Validity

    The validity of swimming lesson is within the duration of a specific swimming batch you are enrolled in. Students may enroll anytime between those dates, provided he/she will finish on the batch’s end date.

  • Continuation of Learning

    Graduates of 8 sessions have the choice to continue their swimming lessons, under our Skills Enhancement Program - whether to polish their chosen swim stroke and prepare for aquathlon/long distance swimming. Valued at Php1,500 for 5 sessions of 1.5 hrs each.
